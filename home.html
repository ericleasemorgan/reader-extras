Distant Reader Toolbox Helper

This is the fledgling home page of a thing called the "Distant Reader Toolbox Helper" -- a Web front-end to the Distant Reader Toolbox.

The purpose of the Helper is to assist you in the process of using & understanding your collection of Distant Reader study carrels -- your "library". The Helper is a front-end to the set of command-line tool that came bundled with the Toolbox. As of right now, there are only a few thing here:

  • home
  • your library
  • table viewer/editor (OpenRefine)
  • indexer/search engine (Solr)
  • database (SQLite)
